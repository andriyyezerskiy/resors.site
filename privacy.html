Privacy Policy — Resors

Effective Date: November 4, 2025

Resors respects your privacy. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Resors does not collect personal information or analytics data. All data and settings you work with remain on your device unless you choose to export or share them manually.

2. Use of iCloud

Resors uses Apple’s iCloud service to securely store and sync user data — such as app preferences or user generated data — across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account, and Resors does not have access to it. Apple’s iCloud terms and privacy policies apply to this data.

3. Data Security

All data processed by Resors stays local to your device or your iCloud account. No data is transmitted to third-party servers or analytics services.

4. Your Choices

You can disable iCloud syncing at any time in your device’s iCloud settings. Doing so will keep all Resors data stored locally on your Mac.
